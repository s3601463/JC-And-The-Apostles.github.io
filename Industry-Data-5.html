    Industry Data

    Communication Skills are clearly the most in demand skill with 9362 jobs posted whilst only 158 jobs posted required listening to be a required skill. Likewise, Management Skills are rather low at 584 jobs posted while there is also a bid demand for Writing Skills with 3614 posted jobs.

    The 3 Highest in demand General Skills not required directly for any of our jobs are Troubleshooting with 2372 jobs posted, Detail orientation with 1812 jobs posted and specifically research skills with 1719 posted jobs in the most recent collection of data.

    After looking at the data provided by the Burning Glass, personally my opinion on my Ideal job is unchanged as apart from just numbers, it doesn’t give much other information on the job and even though there may little to no demand for a senior data scientist (my ideal job), it’s still a job a would love to do and ideally it would probably be my first preference if I had to choose and as previously mentioned, this hasn’t changed.
