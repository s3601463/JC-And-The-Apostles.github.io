    1. Voting systems. Voting for a party or a person in a position of power can be done via a computer or digitally uploaded. When a user places a vote that voters ID gets hashed, and then that data gets stored as completed. The vote choice has an allocated number and is stored encrypted and sent to the server. When the votes are called to view, they are decrypted and then display their values to their respective party/person.
    2. Bitcoin. Bitcoin transaction are completed via blockchain system, transactions are private to the outside however hashing is used to validate the process and complete the transactions.
